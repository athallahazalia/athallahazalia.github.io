Kani Mayo Egg Spread On Toast

by Athallah Azalia - Azel 9C

Ingredients :

  • 1 egg
  • 1 kani stick
  • 1 tbsp mayo
  • 1 tsp chili sauce
  • 2 slice of bread
  • a pinch of salt
  • a pinch of pepper

Tools :

  • pan
  • lid pan
  • toaster
  • cutting board
  • knife
  • plate
  • bowl
  • fork
  • spoon
  • strainer
Steps :
  • First you need to put 1 eggs in a pot and fill it with water. Put it over high heat, cover it with a lid and wait until it comes to a boil. After it comes to boil put it in a bowl and let the eggs sit for about 5 minutes then peel off the shells.
  • Second mix together mayo and chili sauce in a small bowl. After it mix well add salt and pepper to add the flavour and mix it again.
  • Then add 1 kani stick in the pan you used to boil the egg. Wait until it floated. Once they floated to the top of the water, drain well the kani. After that slice your kani into a small pieces with cutting board and knife.
  • After that put the kani sticks into the mayo bowl and mix well. Add your egg and smash it until they are well mixed.
  • Last but not least toast your bread with toaster. After it toasted well put the bread in a plate. Add your Kani Mayo Egg then spread on the top of the bread. You can add parsley flakes if you want
  • Finally your kani mayo egg spread on toast is ready to eat. Enjoy!
